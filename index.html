Elite Controller was designed

to make you play

like a real professional

Designed to meet the needs of competitive gamers, Xbox Elite Wireless Controller Series 2 was built for you to compete at the next level with included interchangeable components that unlock limitless customization in the games you love the most.

Immensely customizable

Optimize the controller for yourself

Experience limitless customization with exclusive button mapping options in the Xbox accessories app. Save up to 3 custom profiles and 1 default profile on the controller and switch between them on the fly with the Profile button.

Switch instantly between devices

Easily pair and switch between Xbox Series X|S and Xbox One consoles, Windows PC, and mobile devices with Xbox Wireless and Bluetooth.

Personalize your own setup

Swap thumbstick toppers, D-pads and paddles to tailor your controller to your preferred gaming style.

Premium materials

Shorter hair trigger locks

New hair locks allow you to pull the trigger halfway. It can be pulled repeatedly faster which results into faster rate of fire.

Wrap-around rubber grip

Allows you to keep your aim firmly on the target without losing it. Good resistance to sweat.

Adjustable thumbsticks

Enhance your aiming. Improved resistance to stick backlash.

Additional accessories

Carrying case

The convenient case lets you carry your gamepad and its accessories without worrying about losing something or keeping it clean.

Charging dock

The charging station allows you to charge the gamepad even when you don't have access to a power outlet, acting as a power bank.

Long battery life

Elite Series 2 is capable of running 30% longer than before. At the same time, the gamepad charges up to 20% faster.
